Howl's Moving Castle

A young girl named Sophie lives a boring life working at her late father's hat shop. However, that all changes when she befriends a wizard named Howl who lives in a magical flying castle. The jealous Witch of the Waste casts a spell on Sophie, turning her into an old woman. Howl, along with his magical friends Markl, Calcifer, and Turnip-Head, go on a quest to defeat the evil witch and restore Sophie's youth.
